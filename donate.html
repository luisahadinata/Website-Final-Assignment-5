WHERE THE MONEY GOES

To make a donation, you can choose between a single donation or pledge a monthly gift. Any type of donation you choose would be highly appreciated and help improve the lives of working animals around the world.

The money that you donate would be use to provide free veterinary care to aid working animals in need. SPANA's team train owners to handle working animals effectively, with care and compassion. We also teach children about animal welfare so that the next generation will learn how to respect and be kind to working animals.
