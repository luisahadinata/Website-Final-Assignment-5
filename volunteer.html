TYPES OF VOLUNTEERING

Behind The Scenes

If you feel like you want to contribute but don't want to get on the field you can do things such as running stalls,attend events, help with administration or even run your own fundrasing event. You don't need to have specific skills to volunteer, just be passionate about helping these animals and you're more than welcome to join!

On The Ground

Help support these animals by doing things such as treating sick working animals or educating children on animal welfare. You'll be involved in training, treating sick working animals, and teaching children on the importance of respecting working animals. Don't worry as we will help and guide you along the way!
