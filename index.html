DID YOU KNOW?

There are around 300 million animals around the world being used for transport and agriculture purposes. Most of these animals are being poorly treated with long working hours and little rest, being denied social and behavioural needs, being kept in poor conditions, and being underfed.

Spana treats more than 388k working animals in need each year. We also provide free treatment for these animals from over 50 veterinary facilities worldwide.
